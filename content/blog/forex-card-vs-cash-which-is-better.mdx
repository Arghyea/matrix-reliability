It's not really cash *or* card — it's how much of each. Here's a quick framework.

## When a forex card wins

- **Locked rate.** You fix your rate the day you load, so currency swings during your trip don't hurt you.
- **Safety.** Lose a card, block it and move the balance. Lose cash, it's gone.
- **Big-ticket spends.** Hotels, tours, and shopping are cleaner on card.

## When cash wins

- **Small vendors, taxis, tips** that don't take cards.
- **Backup** in case of network issues or a blocked card.
- **Remote destinations** with patchy card acceptance.

## A sensible default

For most trips, carry **enough cash for two or three days of small spending**, and put the rest on a forex card. Adjust up on cash for destinations that are cash-heavy.
